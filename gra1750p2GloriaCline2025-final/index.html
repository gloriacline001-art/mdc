Quesillo Venezolano

Here is the recipe for the best quesillo you’ve ever eaten and its oven safe. Made on the stovetop, its unique in its flavor, made with egg and condense milk, making it an irresistible dessert. Don’t miss this recipe to make it just as I explained.

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 1 can condense milk
  • 1 can regular milk(use condense milk can to measure)
  • 1 teaspoon of Vanilla
  • 100 grams sugar(to make the caramel)
  • 1 18cm diameter mold

Steps

  1. To make the caramel add sugar, to the chosen mold and heat it directly on the stove, let it melt moving the mold around so the sugar melts evenly. Remove from the heat,spread the caramel through the mold
  2. In a larg pot that fits the mold, add a litle water and heat ie until it has a lid (this is for double boiler)
  3. In a blender combine: eggs, condense mil, regular milk, vanilla blend until is well blended.
  4. Place the cream in the caramelized cool mold & room temperature.
  5. Place the mold inside the pot with the water heating,cover the pot and let it cook for one hour, over high heat, making sure the pot has enough water but dont let the mold float.
  6. The water should be halfway up the mold, which should be cover, and the pot shall also be cover.
  7. After one hour the quesillo shall be ready.
